About Us

Jackie's Tee Time was born from a love of healthcare and design. We create apparel specifically for medical assistants—comfortable, durable shirts that look great inside and outside the clinic. Our designs celebrate the everyday heroes in healthcare, with subtle details that speak to your work ethic and pride.

Each shirt is printed on demand to reduce waste and ensure you get the size and color that fits you best. Whether you’re working a long shift or enjoying your downtime, we hope our tees make you feel confident and comfortable.

Have an idea for a design or feedback about our products? We’d love to hear from you. Reach out via our contact page or through social media. Thank you for supporting a small business that’s passionate about medical assistants!
